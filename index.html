statement terminator

groovy:

newline is not a separator inside (), [], triple quote literal, or after binary operator or backslash

scala:

newline is not a separator if:
  • the line ends with an infix operator, including a period
  • the following line begins with a word that is not legal at the start of a statement
  • the line ends inside a parens or square brackets, neither of which can contain multiple statements
